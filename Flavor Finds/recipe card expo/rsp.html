Roasted Sweet Potatoes

.


Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Prepare the sweet potatoes.
  3. Season the sweet potatoes.
  4. Roast the sweet potatoes.
  5. Bake and Serve.
